Shipping Policy

We process all orders within 2–3 business days after order confirmation.

Standard Shipping:
- Delivery time: 5–7 business days
- Available across most domestic locations

Express Shipping:
- Delivery time: 1–2 business days
- Additional charges apply

Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on delivery location and shipping method.

Once an order is shipped, tracking information will be shared via email.

We are not responsible for delays caused by external factors such as weather conditions, customs clearance, or courier service disruptions.
